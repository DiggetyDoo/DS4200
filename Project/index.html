Topic:

Strategic analysis of soccer club and player performance across european top leagues

Why it’s interesting:

EA FC24 is the most frequently updating and accessible data platform as it is played and assessed by soccer enthusiasts all over the world. Through this data we have the opportunity to tap into real time data and insights and capture the latest trends and development in the soccer world. Our dynamic analysis of soccer clubs and players performance can provide actionable insights that can be used by club’s management team to improve a club's overall performance through player trading and training.

Task:

  • Data abstraction (Cleaning up the data):
    • Removing non relevant data from the dataset.
    • Sort the players into different fifa versions, leagues, positions.
    • Further filtering the data set to our target leagues (Premier league, Bundesliga, and La Liga)
  • Data Analysis:
    • League analysis: Finding benchmark overall rating for each league to differentiate top players in each league
    • Club analysis: Finding each of club’s player compositions by position in terms of performance attributes to determine player trading and transfers needed.
    • Player search: Finding the suitable player with aligning performance and external factors for a club’s improvement needs
  • Data visualizations:
    • Generate data visualizations for each analysis to get in-depth findings.
